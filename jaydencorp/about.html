Overview

Jayden Corp. International (JCI) is an American-based multinational conglomerate founded in 2018 as a collaborative effort between The Coca-Cola Company (NYSE:KO) and ExxonMobil (NYSE:XOM). JCI’s portfolio encompasses several industries, including information technology, life sciences, investment capital, and research. The company is best known for its innovative research into offshore shale gas fracking and genetically modified carbonated drinks. In November 2018, JCI donated five million USD to the Great America Political Action Committee (PAC).

In December 2018, JCI released the first next-generation intelligent personal assistant, Jayden.
