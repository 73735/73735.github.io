!nnovations

British American Tabacco Japan Intranet Portal.

Click here to see the demo.

Problem

A portal site for internal initiatives for !nnovation was needed for British American Tobacco Japan Office. Users are multinational employees many of whom are new to Japan and Japan office.

Solution

We decided to use the rectangular exclamation mark of the project as our key shape and create a rich interaction experience in order to make visiting the site fun.

The rectangles are stacked like cards for users to flip through and act as portals to enter each area of the site. Each section has a different theme and hidden interactions which users are encourage to explore. The lighting of the site also changes depending on the time of the day.

Expanding animation was added in order to enhance the feeling of the door opening.
