Mitas Medical

Providing medical services where it cannot be reached.

Service

Mitas are targeting rural areas/remote islands/emerging countries around the world where access to ophthalmologists is limited and provide services for prevention and early detection of eye diseases.

In collaboration with Mitas, we designed a telemedicine service "Mobile Eye Doctor" which connects ophthalmologists to those who are in need. A dedicated app with a mounting device enables clinicians other than ophthalmologists to easily take quality pictures and videos of patients' eyes, and send them to ophthalmologists along with guided medical interviews for a consultation.

Information Architecture

Through a series of interviews with the founder who is also an ophthalmologist, we designed the information architecture and the flow for each platform.

Click to Open

Clinic

Clinic staff input patients’ personal information and symptoms into the app. They can also take pictures and videos of patients’ eyes using a special device mounted on a smartphone. Asking questions listed on the app to patients allow them to interview the same way as ophthalmologists usually do. Patients' information, interview results, and pictures and videos of eyes are uploaded to the cloud through the app which can be used to consult with ophthalmologists.

Admin
