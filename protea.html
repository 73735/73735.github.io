Protea Live Active® STORE

Centralizing data insights to optimize efficiency and boost sales for a cosmetics company.

Problem

Protea has been a supplier of Japanese cosmetics products for decades. Yet as the firm sought to expand from a pure supplier B2B model into a direct retail position, it recognized that growth could not be sustained without significant changes to its operating model.

The firm had no integrated supply chain management, no ability to capture or measure data insights on consumers, vendors, and products—and ultimately, no measurement or actions against KPIs.

Protea lacked the ability to analyze which consumers had multiple accounts in different branches, or which products were in the highest demand. They had already spent millions on various technical systems—siloed, incomplete solutions that did not talk to each other. Protea was making both strategic and day-to-day decisions in the dark.

Solution

We created Protea’s first-ever centralized database for customers, products, order history, and vendors, and enabled stakeholders to first understand audience behavior, and then anticipate and act on relevant patterns within that behavior—including campaigns and data capture, loyalty and ambassador programs, e-learning, online B2B orders, and accounting.

EC Site

Renewing Protea’s eCommerce site Live Active® STORE was one of the key tasks in the project as the site accounts for nearly half of the company's revenue.

Information Architecture

We made minimum changes to the search section of the site in order to prevent confusing existing users who are mainly elderly women who are not keen on the latest technology and the majority of the users are returning customers who were familiar with the previous site.

We also conducted extensive secondary research on the competitors’ sites to understand the users’ expectations of how loyalty programs are presented and prepared the My Page section of the site.

Features

We continuously add and remove features based on the business needs and the customer needs.

Style Guide

Working closely with Protea’s internal branding team to learn about their pre-existing materials and direction, we presented a series of mood boards and developed final style guides. We decided to go with a minimalistic black and white style which represents Protea’s high-end branding very well.

We specified every aspect of the site even the spacing to maintain consistency and to simplify the design QA process for dynamically generated pages.

Controlled Vocabulary

Through the secondary research of similar sites, we compiled a list of commonly used vocabularies to optimize the user experience and also to push the branding such as using the term Shopping Bag instead of Shopping Cart.

Native App

The native app was built based on the browser version of the Live Active® STORE. The target users of the app are loyal customers who visit the clinic often in addition to purchasing the products online.

Partner App

Protea’s partner’s app is installed on a tablet distributed to each clinic. Staffers at clinics can scan a QR code on a customer's card to access to customer’s info including purchase history, vouchers, and favorite products. Purchases can be also made by scanning a QR code on a product and purchased products will immediately appear in the order history of the user's app. Staffers can also check the inventory and make an order using the app.
